# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

SecureTech Solutions Ltd. is committed to providing a welcoming, professional, and inclusive environment for all members of our cybersecurity community. We value the participation of every individual and want to ensure that everyone has a positive and respectful experience.

This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for professional conduct and applies to all participants in this project, including contributors, reviewers, and community members.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

We encourage all participants to:

- **Be Respectful** — Treat all individuals with dignity and respect
- **Be Professional** — Maintain a high standard of professional conduct
- **Be Collaborative** — Work together constructively toward common goals
- **Be Inclusive** — Welcome and support colleagues from diverse backgrounds
- **Be Constructive** — Provide feedback that is helpful and actionable
- **Be Patient** — Recognize that we all have different expertise levels
- **Be Accountable** — Take responsibility for your words and actions

### Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

- **Harassment or Discrimination** — Based on race, color, gender, religion, national origin, sexual orientation, gender identity, age, disability, or any other protected characteristic
- **Intimidation or Bullying** — Using aggressive, threatening, or abusive language
- **Unwelcome Advances** — Sexual or romantic advances that are not welcomed
- **Disruptive Conduct** — Deliberately disrupting conversations or sabotaging collaborative work
- **Personal Attacks** — Attacking someone's character, appearance, or background
- **Abuse of Authority** — Misusing power or position to disadvantage others
- **Deliberate Misinformation** — Spreading false or misleading information
- **Illegal Activity** — Any conduct that violates applicable laws

## Specific Guidelines

### In Code Reviews

- Provide constructive feedback focused on the code, not the person
- Ask questions to understand the contributor's perspective
- Acknowledge good work and valid approaches
- Be responsive to reviews of your own contributions

### In Discussions

- Stay on topic
- Disagree respectfully and professionally
- Provide evidence or rationale for your viewpoints
- Listen actively to different perspectives
- Avoid off-topic political or religious discussions

### In Documentation

- Use inclusive and non-discriminatory language
- Avoid stereotypes or assumptions
- Be accurate and cite sources appropriately
- Respect different perspectives while maintaining technical accuracy

## Reporting Concerns

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

### Immediate Concerns
- If you feel personally threatened, contact local authorities immediately

### Project Concerns
- **Report Directly:** Email code-of-conduct@securetech.local with:
  - Description of the incident
  - When and where it occurred
  - Individuals involved
  - Any witnesses
  - Impact on you or others

- **Report to Maintainers:** Contact the project maintainers at jonathan.lisam@securetech.local

### Confidentiality
All reports will be handled with appropriate confidentiality and discretion. We will not disclose reporter identities without consent.

## Investigation Process

1. **Acknowledgment** — We will acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
2. **Investigation** — We will conduct a fair and thorough investigation
3. **Resolution** — We will take appropriate action based on findings
4. **Follow-up** — We will follow up with relevant parties

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

- **Warning** — For minor or first-time violations
- **Temporary Suspension** — For repeated or more serious violations
- **Permanent Removal** — For severe violations or repeated failure to correct behavior

All individuals are held to the same standards, regardless of status or position.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (GitHub, emails, discussions, meetings)
- Official project events
- Informal gatherings related to the project
- Public interactions when representing the project

## Diversity and Inclusion

We are committed to:

- Creating an inclusive environment for people from all backgrounds
- Valuing diverse perspectives and experiences
- Actively working to eliminate bias and discrimination
- Supporting underrepresented groups in cybersecurity
- Providing accommodations for accessibility needs
